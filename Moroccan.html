Moroccan Chickpea Tagine

🌐

Cuisine

African

⏱️

Prep Time

50 minutes

⭐

Difficulty

Easy

👤

Servings

4 Persons

👨‍🍳

Cook Time

30 minutes

Fragrant, hearty, and richly spiced — a North African gem! This Moroccan Chickpea Tagine is the perfect vegan dish packed with vibrant vegetables and warm spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Cooking Instructions.

01

In a large pot, heat some oil and sauté the onion and garlic until softened.

02

Add the cinnamon, cumin, and paprika. Toast the spices briefly until fragrant.

03

Stir in the carrots, zucchini, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer.

04

Let the mixture simmer for 30 minutes until the vegetables soften.

05

Add the chickpeas and cook for an additional 10 minutes.

06

Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
