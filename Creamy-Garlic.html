Let’s Cook

Creamy Garlic
Parmesan Chicken

🌐

Cuisine

Italian

⏱️

Prep Time

35 minutes

⭐

Difficulty

Easy

👤

Servings

2–3 Persons

👨‍🍳

Cook Time

15 minutes

This Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken is a comforting Italian dish with a velvety sauce, bursting with flavor from fresh garlic and rich Parmesan. Perfect to enjoy over pasta, rice, or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Cooking Instructions.

01

Season and cook the sliced chicken breasts in butter until golden on both sides. Remove from the pan and set aside.

02

In the same pan, sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.

03

Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.

04

Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted and well combined.

05

Return the chicken to the pan, coat with the sauce, and simmer for another 10 minutes.

06

Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
