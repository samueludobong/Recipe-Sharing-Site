Punjabi Dal Makhani

🌐

Cuisine

Indian

⏱️

Prep Time

45 minutes

⭐

Difficulty

Intermediate

👤

Servings

4 Persons

👨‍🍳

Cook Time

60 minutes

Rich and buttery, Dal Makhani is a North Indian favorite comfort food. Slow-cooked lentils and beans are simmered with spices, butter, and cream to create a hearty dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole black lentils (sabut urad dal)
  • 1/4 cup kidney beans (rajma)
  • 3 tbsp ghee or butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Cooking Instructions.

01

Soak the black lentils and kidney beans overnight in water.

02

Pressure cook the lentils and beans with water for 30-40 minutes until tender.

03

Heat ghee or butter in a pan, add cumin seeds, and sauté until fragrant.

04

Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates.

05

Stir in spices (turmeric, garam masala, red chili powder, coriander) and cook for 2 minutes.

06

Add the cooked dal (lentils and beans) and simmer on low heat for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

07

Finish with a generous splash of cream, and garnish with cilantro.
