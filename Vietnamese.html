Vietnamese Pho Bo

🌐

Cuisine

Vietnamese

⏱️

Prep Time

1 hour

⭐

Difficulty

Intermediate

👤

Servings

4 Persons

👨‍🍳

Cook Time

30 minutes

A fragrant, flavorful noodle soup with tender beef slices and aromatic herbs — a Vietnamese classic!

Ingredients

  • 300g beef brisket, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 1 ginger root, sliced
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • Rice noodles
  • Herbs: basil, cilantro, bean sprouts, lime

Cooking Instructions.

01

Bring a pot of water to boil and add onion, ginger, cinnamon, and star anise.

02

Add beef brisket and cook for 10 minutes. Season with fish sauce.

03

Prepare noodles according to package directions.

04

Assemble pho with noodles, beef, broth, and fresh herbs.
