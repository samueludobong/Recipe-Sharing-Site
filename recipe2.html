Creamy Chicken Parmesan

🌐

Cuisine

Italian

⏱️

Prep Time

10 minutes

⭐

Difficulty

Medium

👤

Servings

4 Persons

👨‍🍳

Cook Time

40 minutes

A creamy and cheesy take on classic chicken parmesan that’s sure to satisfy your cravings!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (preferably panko)
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Cooking Instructions.

01

Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).

02

Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Dredge each piece in flour, dip in beaten eggs, then coat with a mixture of breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese.

03

Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the chicken for 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.

04

In a separate saucepan, heat the marinara sauce and stir in the heavy cream.

05

Place the cooked chicken breasts in a baking dish. Pour the creamy marinara sauce over the chicken and top with shredded mozzarella.

06

Bake for 20-25 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

07

Garnish with fresh basil and serve with pasta, rice, or a side of vegetables.
