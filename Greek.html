Greek Lemon Chicken Souvlaki

🌐

Cuisine

Mediterranean

⏱️

Prep Time

35 minutes

⭐

Difficulty

Easy

👤

Servings

4 Persons

👨‍🍳

Cook Time

15 minutes

Zesty, herby, and so fresh — a perfect summer grill recipe for juicy chicken bites!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, cubed
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Skewers

Cooking Instructions.

01

Marinate chicken with lemon juice, oregano, olive oil, salt, and pepper for 30 minutes.

02

Skewer the marinated chicken pieces.

03

Grill or pan-cook until browned and cooked through.

04

Serve with tzatziki sauce.
